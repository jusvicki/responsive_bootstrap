ABOUT

Traditions of innovation, excellence and grandeur imbue Ralph&co women’s watches with horological pedigree like no other. Hand-crafted by master watchmakers in Geneva, Patek Philippe timepieces symbolize the prestigious history of Swiss watchmaking down to the last detail. The Maison has supplied some of the world’s finest watches to royalty and aristocracy since 1839. As one of the longest-operating watch manufacturers in the world, Ralph&co is renowned for its luxurious watches and intricate complications. Each built with the finest metals, diamonds and semi-precious materials, ladies’ Patek Philippe watches typify the house’s commitment to haute horlogerie.
